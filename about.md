## Willams de Lima, M.Sc.

Hello there! I have a M.Sc. in Computer Science from Federal University of Pernambuco (2020), advised by prof. Veronica Teichrieb and prof. Lucas Silva Figueiredo, and a B.Sc. in Computer Engineering from Mauricio de Nassau University (2018), advised by prof. Lucas Silva Figueiredo. I expect to receive my PhD in 2024.

I research Deep Learning and Computer Vision focused to Human-Computer Interaction at Voxar Labs, a research laboratory in the state of Pernambuco, Brazil. My interests are (1) applying deep learning to provide (a more) natural interaction between humans and machines; (2) investigate and tackle problems in biomedical engineering (specially in imaging) using deep and machine learning; (3) apply core deep learning concepts to solve real world problems.

I contribute to the community by reviewing research works for publication in conferences such as SVR (2018, 2019, 2020) and journals such as IEEE Access (2019, 2020). I also could contributed by acting as Web chair of ISMAR (2020) and SVR (2020) and Web support in SIBGRAPI (2020).
